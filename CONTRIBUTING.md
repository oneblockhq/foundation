# Contributing to ONE BLOCK LTD

Welcome to the **ONE BLOCK** community! We are thrilled that you want to help us build the foundational blocks of the future.

## Our Philosophy
We value quality over quantity. Every contribution should adhere to our **Core Engineering Principles**.

## How to Contribute

### 1. Identify a Block
Check our open issues or projects. If you have a new idea, please open a **Feature Request** or **RFC** (Request for Comments).

### 2. Craft Your Solution
- Follow our coding standards (refer to `docs/CORE_PRINCIPLES.md`).
- Ensure your code is well-documented and covered by tests.
- Maintain a focus on premium UI/UX if working on frontend components.

### 3. Review & Refine
- Submit a Pull Request with a clear description and a mention of the related issue.
- Be prepared for a constructive review process. We believe in helping each other improve.

## Community Standards
We are committed to building a positive, inclusive, and professional tech community. Please respect all members and focus on collaborative growth.

## Contact
For specific inquiries or collaboration opportunities, reach out to us at [oneblockhq@gmail.com](mailto:oneblockhq@gmail.com).
